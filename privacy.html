Gmail TL;DR — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Gmail TL;DR (“we”, “our”, or “the extension”) is a Chrome extension that provides AI-generated summaries of Gmail messages using the Google Gemini API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the extension.

2. What Information We Collect

a. Email Content (Temporarily Accessed)

  • What: Visible content from the currently open email thread in your Gmail tab.
  • How: Extracted directly from your browser via a content script, only when the extension is triggered.
  • Purpose: Used solely to generate a concise summary using the Gemini API.
  • Storage: Not stored permanently or transmitted to any server other than the Gemini API endpoint.

b. User Preferences (Optional)

  • What: Basic extension preferences (e.g., visibility settings).
  • How: Stored using Chrome’s local storage API.
  • Purpose: To remember your UI preferences between sessions.
  • Storage: Persisted locally in your browser until cleared or the extension is uninstalled.

3. Use of Third-Party Services

The extension uses Google’s Gemini API to process email content. Google’s privacy policy applies to that usage.

Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

  • All communication with Gemini API is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • No data is stored externally.
  • No cookies or tracking scripts are used.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, or trade user data.
  • Email content is used solely for summarization with Gemini API.

6. Children’s Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your Rights

  • Uninstall the extension at any time.
  • Clear all extension-stored preferences from browser settings.
  • Contact us for privacy-related inquiries.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
